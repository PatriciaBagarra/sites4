Boost Your Digital Marketing

We Keep Your Digital Ads In Front Of Targeted Consumers, Nonstop

At Genius Monkey, our programmatic marketing technology is designed to attract your target consumers and advance them through our sales funnel. We do this through our attribution and tracking operations, keeping our clients’ digital ads in front of targeted consumers on all of their device screens, channels, and favorite websites. Accurately identifying and targeting your quality audience is paramount to the Genius Monkey digital marketing platform. Without these measures, brands market to mass general audiences without any guarantee or detailed tracking points to ensure eventual conversions.

Convert Existing Site Visitors by Making it Personal

On average, 96% of visitors to a website end up leaving before converting. For this reason, marketing follow-through and tenacity are critical for brands who wish to succeed. At Genius Monkey, our advanced tracking and attribution technology sets us apart from other programmatic marketing platforms because we’re able to respond immediately to the interests and behaviors of each individual site visitor. Our digital advertising platform combines the capability of retargeting and dynamic ads to create a powerful personalized marketing experience for your target customers.

How do We Follow Digital Leads?

When a prospect visits your website, our digital marketing platform analyzes their behavior, including pages that they visit, emails they send and receive, products that they add to their cart, past performance and so much more. If they leave your website without converting, our tracking system can target them across the web on their favorite sites, channels, ad networks, social media, and much more. We run dynamic ads to drive them where they need to be in order to bring a conversion to fruition. Our programmatic marketing platform reaches your audience through an effective combination of email, web, social, and digital ads. If they leave the site, we stick with them. Sometimes they convert quickly, and sometimes it can take months, but we don’t give up!
